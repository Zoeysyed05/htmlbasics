What is HTML?

HTML, or Hypertext Markup Language,is not a scripting language;it's a markup language!

Top 3 Fun Facts about HTML:

  1. HTML was created by Tim Berners-Lee in 1991
  2. HTML is the skeleton of every webpage you see on the internet
  3. HTML5 introduced the article and sections tags for better senantics
